OPTICAL FIBER SYSTEM

OPTICAL FIBER:-
An optical fiber is a stand of dielectric meterial that can carry light from one end two another.
STRUCTURE:-
There are three layer in fiber-
(1) core(light guiding region)
(2) cladding(protect the outer surface of core)
(3) sheath(maintain the uniforrmity along the length of fiber)
TYPES OF OPTICAL FIBER-
(i) Glass optical fiber
(ii) Plastic optical fiber
(iii) Plastic clad silica optical fiber

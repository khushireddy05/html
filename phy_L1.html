PHYSICAL QUANTITY
 SCALER QUATITY-A scaler quatity is a quantity in which only magnitude but not direction is called scaler quantity.
example-tempreture,current,mass etc
VECTER QUATITY-A vecter quatity is a quatity in which magnitude and direction both is called vecter quatity.
example-displacement,velocity,force etc
